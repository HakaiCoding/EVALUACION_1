Dragon Ball Z: Dragon Fist Explosion!

Release Date: July 15, 1995

Dragon Ball Z: Dragon Fist Explosion!! If Gokū Can't Do It, Who Will? (ドラゴンボールＺ 龍拳爆発！！悟空がやらねば誰がやる, Doragon Bōru Zetto Ryū-Ken Bakuhatsu!! Gokū ga Yaraneba Dare ga Yaru), known as Dragon Ball Z: Wrath of the Dragon in the English release, is the sixteenth overall Dragon Ball film, and the thirteenth to be released under the brand Dragon Ball Z. It was originally released in Japan on July 15, 1995 at the Toei Anime Fair.

Designed by Victor Sanchez
